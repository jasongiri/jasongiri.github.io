About the Artist

From the band room to the beat.

JUICD is a high school musician (Class of 2028/2029) and a dedicated Bb clarinet player in both his school's marching band and concert band.

By day he's drilling formations and reading sheet music; by night he's a passionate digital music producer, channeling the discipline and musicality of traditional band into modern electronic and hip-hop beats.

His debut album “CD” is the product of that fusion — classically-trained ears meeting a producer's instinct for rhythm, energy, and sound design.
